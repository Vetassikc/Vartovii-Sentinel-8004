## Longer LinkedIn Post

Most teams in AI trading build the agent.

For the ERC-8004 challenge, we focused on the control layer in front of the
agent.

Sentinel-8004 is a signed trade-permit guardrail for autonomous trading
systems:

- trade intent comes in
- Sentinel evaluates policy and risk
- the system returns `ALLOW`, `DENY`, or `ALLOW_WITH_DOWNSIZE`
- proof is preserved through typed data, signature recovery, validation
  artifacts, permit verification, and organizer-aligned shared-Sepolia anchors

This is not another trading dashboard. It is trust and control infrastructure
for autonomous capital.
